   **large** — folder (`blueprint.md` + appendices):
   - All medium content, 40+ nodes total
   - `metric` + `chart` nodes (financials/projections)
   - Full risk matrix, an architecture `text` node (ASCII/description)
   - `agent` and `cost` nodes where relevant; PDF-export-ready structure

   **continuous** — living version of any tier:
   - Auto-append nodes at decision/assumption/risk events
   - Decision log and version log grow over time
   - Re-run interrogation step 5 (assumptions) at each revisit

   After producing: list the open assumptions and the single most important
   next action. Persist (API or file). Offer to open it on the canvas.
