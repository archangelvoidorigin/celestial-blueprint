## Objective
Create the three reference files used by the skill.

## Instructions
1. Create `skill/celestial-blueprint/reference/interrogation.md` with exactly:
   ```markdown
   # Interrogation Question Bank (BLANK SLATE)

   Use as many as needed; one focused question at a time. Reflect back before
   advancing. Steps 1–3 are discovery only — no judging or planning.

   ## Step 1 — Identity & terrain
   - In one sentence, what is this?
   - Who is it for, and what do they do today instead?
   - What already exists (code, docs, prior attempts)?
   - Hard constraints: deadline, budget, team size, required tech?
   - What is explicitly NOT changing?

   ## Step 2 — Vision & architecture
   - What does "great" look like 3 months out?
   - What are the major moving parts? How do they connect?
   - What is out of scope for this round?
   - Where does this plug into existing systems?

   ## Step 3 — Priorities, wounds & patterns
   - If you could only ship one thing, what is it?
   - What has failed or hurt before here?
   - What patterns/preferences should I respect (or avoid)?
   - What would make you abandon this?

   ## Step 4 — Research (2 queries)
   - Name the two unknowns most likely to change the plan.
   - Research each; capture as `research` nodes with sources + confidence.

   ## Step 5 — Dangerous assumptions (3)
   - List the 3 beliefs that, if false, break the plan.
   - For each: a falsifier (the concrete test that would disprove it).

   ## Step 6 — Loopholes (3)
   - 3 ways this fails, gets gamed, or is exploited.
   - Each becomes a risk-register entry.
   ```
2. Create `skill/celestial-blueprint/reference/chronicle-raw.md` with exactly:
   ```markdown
   # RAW — <trigger> — <YYYY-MM-DD>

   **Context:** <what was happening>
   **Event:** <what triggered this — one of the 7 triggers>
   **Impact:** <nodes / assumptions / decisions / risks touched and how>
   ```
3. Create `skill/celestial-blueprint/reference/chronicle-cycle.md` with exactly:
   ```markdown
   # CYCLE <n> — session <NNN> — <YYYY-MM-DD>

   **Goal:** <what this session set out to plan>

   **What we learned:**
   - <bullet>
   - <bullet>

   **Decisions:**
   - <id> — <decision> — <status>

   **Open assumptions / risks (carried forward):**
   - <item>

   **Version:** bumped to <x.y.z>
   **Next session:** <the single next action>
   ```
